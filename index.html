MyShelf Privacy Policy

MyShelf does not collect or store user data. Our application's core features are provided free of charge, and we do not request personal information from our users.

MyShelf includes a premium membership system that offers additional features to users. Premium memberships are provided through the App Store, and payment transactions are handled entirely through the secure payment systems of the App Store. MyShelf does not collect or store user payment information.

This Privacy Policy and Disclosure Statement may be updated to reflect changes in our application or to comply with legal requirements. We will use appropriate means to inform you of any changes after an update.
